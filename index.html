This website was created to present our second-year project as students in the BUT GEII program of Cergy, at the Sarcelles campus.

Our project: An intelligent noise-canceling headset.

This project aims to help people with concentration and attention disorders, as well as those who experience stress due to their environment.

We will briefly explain our development idea to explore its full potential and see how all our ideas could be realized.

Our project consists of linking a heart rate sensor with a noise-canceling headset.

By this, I mean a heart rate sensor worn on the wrist, which will be connected to the headset via Bluetooth connected grace ESP32.

The headset will then receive the user's data and react accordingly: the noise cancellation will be influenced by the heartbeats.

A program will adjust the noise cancellation level by increasing or decreasing it based on the heart rate.

We came up with the idea of the smart headset by combining the concept of a noise-canceling
headset and making it intelligent, to help people with concentration disorders and stress bursts
A traditional noise-canceling headset is manually adjustable over a short frequency range.
Our project aims to extend this range significantly and have it adjust automatically based
on the wearer's BPM from the smartwatch. This will allow adaptation to any situation the wearer may face."
